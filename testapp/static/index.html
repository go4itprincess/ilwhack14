MSP Involvement

MSP Involvment provides an interactive and intuitive interface to jointly explore geographical data and parliamentary proceedings. This application presents the data from the Scottish Index of Multiple Deprivation index and Scottish Parliament proceedings. Select from the buttons at the top to show a representation of the different Scottish Parliament constituencies across several dimensions. Together with this data you will see a textual analysis of all the interventions in parliamentary debates of the representatives (Members of the Scottish Parliament) for the constituency and of its containing region.
